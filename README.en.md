**Systems Architect for Extreme Environments**

I design open-source infrastructure for critical conditions — from dense cities to Mars.

## What I do

- **Resilient communications** — systems that keep data flowing even when satellites or submarine cables fail ([Quantum-Flux](https://github.com/enriqueherbertag-lgtm/quantum-flux)).
- **Autonomous energy & life support** — urban smog towers ([ShieldAir-Urban](https://github.com/enriqueherbertag-lgtm/ShieldAir-Urban)) and Martian oxygen production ([ShieldAir-Mars](https://github.com/enriqueherbertag-lgtm/ShieldAir-Mars)).
- **Artificial bodies & neural interfaces** — a physical platform for AI or human control ([CORPUS](https://github.com/enriqueherbertag-lgtm/Corpus), [ENA](https://github.com/enriqueherbertag-lgtm/ENA)).

## A concrete example

**Quantum-Flux** is a fault-tolerant communication layer that splits data across multiple satellite links. Even if 2–3 links fail, the message gets through. Designed for governments, banks, and critical infrastructure.

## Philosophy

"Repair the world. Don't replace it."

I build systems that work with existing infrastructure: satellites already in orbit, buildings already in cities, algorithms already proven.
